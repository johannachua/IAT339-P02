about

our company

What started as a post-secondary project became a business organized and managed by three friends. You Were Here is a postcard selling service where people can purchase a variety of unique postcards and postage stamps for their travelling or personal needs. You can personalize every postcard with your own personal message on our website and have your postcard sent by us. Or, the postcards can be sent to you for you to complete on your own!

meet the team

Rupneet Atwal

Traveling and visiting new and exciting places is a clear passion of Rupneet. While on her travels, she enjoys collecting and gifting priceless momentos as a reminder of her travels and memories. Through You Are Here, Rupneet hopes to share her enthusiasm for collecting physical memories and being able to share them with others.

Johanna Chua

As an avid stationary collector, Johanna enjoys browsing online and in-stores for charming paper crafts to add to her collection. She takes pleasure in receiving postcards or any personalized letters from her friends and family. Thus, she hopes that You Were Here. will provide a quick way to bring delightful experiences to others.

Matthew Fong

Matthew Fong is a user experience and user interface designer situated in the Vancouver, Canada area. His passion lies in user research. As someone who enjoys personal understanding and reflection, user research allows him not only to exert empathy and learn about individuals, but to learn more about himself in the process. As a third culture child, Matthew strive to gain a better understanding of him as both ethnically Chinese and a culturally Canadian citizen and how he fits in Western society while being a designer.
